Mission

Pixel Purrfect Arcade is a non-profit dedicated to finding loving homes for stray cats by integrating them into a community-focused, vintage game store and lounge. Our mission is to provide a unique, engaging environment where patrons can enjoy video games, buy or sell them, and interact with cats in need of adoption. We aim to reduce the number of homeless cats in San Francisco's Chinatown while fostering a sense of community and nostalgia through gaming. We provide a forever home for any cat with the hope that they will one day be adopted. We are a no-kill shelter that believes in indoor cats, spayed or neutered, vaccinated, and never declawed. The game lounge operates as a space for cats to socialize with guests and each other as they are able. It is our intention to provide them with the highest quality of life while in our care. While ideally a temporary haven, Pixel Purrfect Arcade has a place for every cat and kitten.

About

Brittany Blaiddyd had captured a stray cat when she ran into a problem: there was nowhere to take him. She couldn't afford to take in another cat, he was unneutered and unvaccinated, and he had sustained injuries from presumed cat fights. Every shelter was full except for a county kill shelter. After finally getting him neutered, vaccinated, and healed, she had no choice but to release him back into the wild. Brittany vowed to improve the shelter capacity for cats in her area by opening a safe haven herself. Pixel Purrfect Arcade is her labor of love for the unwanted and stray cats of the Bay Area.
